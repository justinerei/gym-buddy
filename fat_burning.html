Fat Burning Workouts

Burn calories and shed fat with these effective workouts

Circuit Training

A full-body circuit to keep your metabolism high and burn fat.

  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 15 reps per leg
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
Cardio Workout

A cardio-focused workout to keep your heart rate up and burn fat.

  • Running: 30 minutes
  • Cycling: 20 minutes
  • Jump Rope: 15 minutes
  • Rowing: 20 minutes
Strength and Cardio Mix

Combine strength training and cardio for optimal fat burning.

  • Kettlebell Swings: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Squat to Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Treadmill Sprints: 10 sets of 1 minute
  • Box Jumps: 3 sets of 15 reps
Tabata Workout

An intense workout protocol to burn fat in a short period.

  • Squat Jumps: 8 sets of 20 seconds
  • Push-Ups: 8 sets of 20 seconds
  • Burpees: 8 sets of 20 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 8 sets of 20 seconds
